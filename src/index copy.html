    Roadmap

    Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the famous Semantics, large language ocean and many more stuff and more more more

    step one

    MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT

    The initial goal is to finalize the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to provide the first, basic version of The District VR.

    step two

    EARLY ACCESS ALPHA

    We will thus focus on the next version with various improvements including traditional account management and payment systems as well as blockchain tools to build a connection to the LUKSO network.

    step three

    EARLY ACCESS BETA

    At this stage we will provide new NFT event locations, improved multiplayer capabilities and a browser extension for non-VR users. Also, LUKSO's Universal Profiles will be fully integrated.

    step four

    LAUNCH

    With launch we will focus on interoperability with market-leading VR devices as well as on the integration of the TDVR token that will be used for various purposes on the platform.
