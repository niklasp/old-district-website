Feel teleported to the most exciting party districts in the world and experience the best of club and live music in Virtual Reality.

A space in the metaverse

where everybody can perform DJ sets and concerts

in front of live audiences

with no additional music equipment required.

Meet friends and enjoy live events from your favorite stars or run the show yourself. Explore exclusive content and the possibilities of the metaverse.

DJ SET

Learn how to DJ or use your skills to run a party yourself. State-of-the-art DJ equipment, including familiar faders, buttons and jog wheels, is virtually integrated to mix the music.

VOCAL PERFORMANCE

Grab the mic. Show your skills. Host own events. Perform live.

RUN YOUR OWN CLUB

Buy or rent event locations as NFTs to create own parties, concerts and events.

REAL OWNERSHIP

User accounts and digital assets of TDVR can be stored and managed via LUKSO's blockchain-based Universal Profiles providing users with true ownership.

NFT GALLERY

Virtual art within the environments can be shown, purchased and stored as NFTs on the blockchain.

AVATAR CREATOR

Be who you want to be, regardless of where you come from or what you look like. Create your virtual twin or express yourself in new ways. Digitize yourself from one picture using TDVR's direct-to-avatar solution.

Roadmap

step one

MINIMUM VIABLE PRODUCT

The initial goal is to finalize the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to provide the first, basic version of The District VR.

step two

EARLY ACCESS ALPHA

We will thus focus on the next version with various improvements including traditional account management and payment systems as well as blockchain tools to build a connection to the LUKSO network.

step three

EARLY ACCESS BETA

At this stage we will provide new NFT event locations, improved multiplayer capabilities and a browser extension for non-VR users. Also, LUKSO's Universal Profiles will be fully integrated.

step four

LAUNCH

With launch we will focus on interoperability with market-leading VR devices as well as on the integration of the TDVR token that will be used for various purposes on the platform.
