TDVR (Early Access Beta) will be released in 2022.

In the beginning the music genres most heavily featured will beTechno, Hip Hop and various styles of related club music. Over time though the taste of the community will prevail and there will be multiple kinds of clubs, event spaces and styles of music.

A VR headset like HTC VIVE, HTC VIVE PRO, HTC VIVE COSMOS, Oculus Rift, Oculus Rift S, Oculus Quest and Quest 2 (with link cable), Valve Index and Microsoft Mixed Reality headsets among others are recommended. If new headsets are released TDVR will be adapted to them as well.

(Even if we advise using VR headsets, a browser integration enables non-VR users to participate in TDVR as well.)

TDVR is a Free To Play Experience. Users will only have to pay for special events, exclusive gaming assets and additional options.

We believe in a decentralized internet of permissionless blockchains.User accounts and digital assets (gaming assets, skins, NFTs etc.) of TDVR are stored on the LUKSO Blockchain giving users total ownership of their assets.

Not for now but it`s possible that a token will be integrated at a laterstage.
